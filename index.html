Our Programs

At Progressive Therapeutic Services (PTS)

We specialize in supporting individuals with intellectual & developmental disabilities by providing pathways to greater independence and personal growth. Our cornerstone program, the Community Day Services (CDS), is designed for adults 18 years and older who have developmental disabilities and varying levels of skill development.

Through CDS, we create a supportive, home-like environment where clients can thrive. Our focus is on helping clients build the skills they need for daily living, employment readiness, and community integration — all while receiving individualized care and support. Each program is goal-oriented and tailored to meet the unique needs of every client.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of compassionate professionals who are deeply committed to making a difference. With backgrounds in social work, behavioral health, counseling, and direct support services, each team member brings a unique skill set to the table — but we all share one common goal: helping individuals reach their full potential.

We are more than just staff; we are mentors, advocates, and partners in each client’s success story. From case managers and direct support staff to administrative leadership, every member of our team plays a vital role in delivering high-quality Progressive Therapeutic Services.
