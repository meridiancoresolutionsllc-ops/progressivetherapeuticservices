Client Spotlight: Eddy H.

Eddy has been with PTS for 6 years. During his years here, he has been able to dress how he wants and be who he is.

He’s extremely thankful to all the staff who make him feel comfortable and accepted every day. Being the first openly gay male of PTS, he hopes that it will empower others to come out and be their true selves.
