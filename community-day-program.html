Community Day Program

A home-like environment where clients can thrive.

Structured Daily Routine: Monday through Friday from 8:30 am to 1:30 pm.

Culinary Skills

Weekly cooking and baking activities not only teachable but valuable life skills.

Pet Therapy Program

Participants take part in caring for and training therapy dogs, enriching both their lives and those of the pets.

Art Therapy

Weekly participation with an Art therapist.

Music Therapy

Weekly participation. Participants work with a Music teacher on an array of music activities. Participants participate in singing, karaoke, recital performances to the introduction of playing different instrument.

Community Engagement

Participating in outings, we practice health and wellness with daily exercising and teach financial literacy, Independence and Sexual Education. This includes but not limited to Job Readiness and Life Skill training.

Gardening

Fundamentals of Gardening, planting and growing flowers, vegetables.

Daily Clients Free Choice

Daily participants can participate weekly in open gym, computer skill training, and more. This activity is meant for clients to participate in whatever activity of their choice they would like to be involved in while at the day program.
